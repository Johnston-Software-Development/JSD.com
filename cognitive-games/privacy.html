We collect the following types of information when you use the App:

2. How We Use the Collected Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To Provide App Functionality: To allow you to log in, save your progress, and access your account across devices.

  • To Improve Our App: We use aggregated, anonymized performance data to understand how the mini-games are being used and to identify areas for improvement in game design and app performance.

3. Data Storage and Third-Party Services

The App is built using Flutter and relies on Firebase for its backend infrastructure:

  • Firebase Authentication: Used to manage user accounts (email/password, Google sign-in). Firebase processes and stores your login credentials securely on our behalf.
  • Firebase Firestore/Database: Used to store your game statistics, trend data, and (if opted-in) leaderboard scores. This data is associated with your unique Firebase User ID.

We do not control the data centers or security protocols of these third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of Google and Firebase for more details on their data handling practices.

4. User Controls and Leaderboard Opt-in

Your participation in the community leaderboard is strictly voluntary and controlled by you:

  • Opt-In Required: No personal or performance data is shared publicly on the leaderboard unless you explicitly opt in to participate within the App settings.
  • Anonymous Option: If you opt into the leaderboard, you have the option to remain anonymous. In this case, your ranking will be displayed using a randomly generated identifier instead of any information that could identify you.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt-out of the leaderboard at any time. When you opt-out, your scores and identifier are removed from public view.
